B.E/B.Tech Admission Form

Eligibility & other details

Application Form - B.E/B.TechCandidates who have passed PUC in Science / 10+2 (CBSE/ICSE) or equivalent Examination and obtained 45% of marks in aggregate in three subjects of Physics, Mathematics and any one of the subjects namely, Chemistry, Biology, Bio-Technology, Computer Science & Electronics.
Application forms can be had from the office of the Special Officer, Ghousia Industrial & Engineering Trust, Hosur Road, Near Christ College, Bangalore-29 or from the Office of the Principal, Ghousia College of Engineering, Ramanagaram, on payment of Rs.500/- (Rupees five hundred only) by D.D. drawn in favour of Principal, Ghousia College of Engineering, payable at Ramanagaram.

Application Form - M.Tech in Manufacturing Science & Engineering (Dept. of ME), Machine Design (Dept. of ME), Power System Engineering (Dept. of EE&E), Structural Engineering (Dept. of Civil).
